Our History

Experience a one-of-a-kind coffee alternative made from roasted corn, capturing the essence of a traditional brew but with a twist. Our corn coffee is a caffeine-free, naturally sweet, and carries a smooth, nutty flavor that's perfect for any time of the day.

Our corn coffee is crafted by carefully roasting select corn kernels to bring out their natural flavors. Ground to a fine texture, it's ready to be brewed like your favorite coffee - no special equipment needed.

Corn coffee is more than just a business. it's sustainable solution. By utilizing locally grown corn, we reduce the carbon footprint associated with coffee imports and support the livelihoods Filipino farmers. it's a win-win for both the economy and the environment.

Mission

To create high-quality, sustainable corn coffee that promotes health, supports local farmers, and provides a unique, caffeine-free alternative to traditional coffee, fostering a meaningful connection with consumers through our commitment to natural ingredients and cultural heritage.

Vision

To become a global leader in alternative coffee beverages by pioneering the corn coffee market, recognized for our innovation, sustainability, and support for local agricultural communities, inspiring a shift toward diverse and eco-friendly drink options that celebrate traditional flavors.
